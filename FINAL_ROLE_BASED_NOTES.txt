Best demo flow:
1. Login as Admin/Registrar -> create a new course or send announcement.
2. Login as HOD -> assign faculty, room and timing to a course.
3. Login as Student -> enroll course; dashboard, schedule and course registration update.
4. Login as Faculty -> open Courses page and see enrolled students.
5. Faculty posts assignment/announcement.
6. Login as Student -> assignment/announcement appears; submit assignment.
7. Login as Faculty -> submission appears; add marks/feedback.
8. Student checks assignment again and sees checked result.
